NFTPER offers bots that can do a variety of automated tasks for you. We are a new start-up focused on bringing automation to the NFT flipping space.

Mass offers

Send offers to each NFT in a collection. You can also send offers to NFTs with specific traits or names

Collection offers

Send collection offers based on the margin or the prices you specify; if someone bids higher, the bot will automatically outbid them.
