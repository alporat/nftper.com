NFTPER offers bots that can do a variety of automated tasks for you. We are a new start-up focused on bringing automation to the NFT flipping space. The roadmap and documentation will be available soon.

Made for NFT flippers, by an NFT flipper.

Collection offers

Send collection offers based on the margin or the prices you specify; if someone bids higher, the bot will automatically outbid them.

Mass offers

Send offers to each NFT in a collection. Set the amount to offer on specific traits, and skip those with/without specific traits.

List-detecting offers

Send an offer to an NFT immediately after it has been listed in the specified collection. Has the same specifications as the Mass offer.
